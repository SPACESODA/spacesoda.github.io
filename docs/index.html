News and Press

Are you a wordsmith looking for a new challenge? Do you enjoy creative writing and competing with others? Look no further than Prompt League!

Prompt League is an exciting new Discord-based competition that allows prompters to battle against each other and AI to see who can come up with the best response. Each round, participants will be given a prompt and two minutes to modify it to yield a better response. They will then generate a response based on their modified prompt, and the responses will be judged by a separate Chat GPT to determine the winner.

The prompts cover a wide range of topics, from science and technology to history and literature. Not only is Prompt League a fun and engaging way to sharpen your writing skills, but it's also a great way to expand your knowledge and creativity.

Whether you're a seasoned writer or just starting out, Prompt League is the perfect way to challenge yourself and improve your writing. So why not join us today and see what all the excitement is about?
